Who We Are

Hands-on, from quote to install.

Wraphix is a Kitchener-based large-format printing and vinyl wrap company. No call centres, no account managers passing you around — you work directly with the people designing and installing your project.

Our Story

Built on real jobs, not stock photos.

Wraphix started with a simple idea: if a surface can be printed and wrapped, we can make it look better — a delivery van, a tired storefront, an empty office wall. Every project on this site is a real install, not a rendering.

Design runs through CorelDraw in-house, printing happens on commercial-grade large-format equipment, and installation is handled by a team that's done everything from single work vans to full multi-storey building takeovers with lift equipment.

How We Work

What you can expect.

Fast, Honest Turnaround

Most quotes come back in 2–5 business days. If a job needs longer, we tell you up front — no radio silence.

Mockup Before Production

You see a visual mockup before we cut a single sheet of film. What you approve is what gets installed.

One Team, Start to Finish

Design, printing, and installation stay in-house — no handoffs between separate vendors and no lost details.

Work That Holds Up

Commercial-grade film, proper surface prep, and clean installs — built to hold colour and adhesion through Ontario weather.
